Title: Right to Information (RTI)
Overview: The RTI Act, 2005 empowers Indian citizens to seek information from public authorities, making the government more accountable and transparent.
Eligibility: Any citizen of India can file an RTI request.
Required Documents:
- No specific documents required for the application.
- Proof of BPL (Below Poverty Line) category to waive fees.
Fees:
- Application Fee: ₹10.
- Photocopy Charges: ₹2 per page.
- Inspection of Records: No fee for the first hour; ₹5 for each subsequent hour.
Steps to File Online:
1. Visit the RTI Online portal.
2. Click on 'Submit Request'.
3. Select the Ministry/Department/Public Authority.
4. Fill the personal details and the specific information sought.
5. Pay the ₹10 fee online (waived for BPL).
6. Submit and note the registration number.
Processing Time: Information must be provided within 30 days (48 hours if it concerns life or liberty).
Official Portal: https://rtionline.gov.in
Sources: https://rtionline.gov.in/about-us.php
Authority: Department of Personnel and Training, Government of India.
