Title: Permanent Account Number (PAN) Card
Overview: PAN is a unique 10-character alphanumeric identifier, issued as a laminated card, by the Income Tax Department.
Eligibility: Any person, company, or entity can apply for a PAN card. It is mandatory for taxpayers and for various financial transactions.
Required Documents: 
- Proof of Identity: Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport.
- Proof of Address: Aadhaar Card, Electricity Bill, Bank Statement.
- Date of Birth Proof: Birth Certificate, 10th Class Certificate.
Fees: 
- Physical PAN Card: ₹107 (within India), ₹1017 (outside India).
- e-PAN Card: ₹72.
Steps to Apply:
1. Visit the NSDL (now Protean) or UTIITSL website.
2. Select Form 49A for Indian Citizens.
3. Fill in the details and upload documents.
4. Pay the fee online.
5. Send the acknowledgment and documents to the income tax office (if not using Aadhaar-based e-KYC).
Processing Time: 15 to 20 working days.
Official Portal: https://www.incometax.gov.in
Sources: https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/help/permanent-account-number
Authority: Income Tax Department, Government of India.
