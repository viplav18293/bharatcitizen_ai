Title: Voter ID Card (EPIC)
Overview: The Elector's Photo Identity Card (EPIC) is an identity document issued by the Election Commission of India to adult citizens.
Eligibility: Indian citizens aged 18 years or above on the qualifying date (1st January of the year).
Required Documents:
- Proof of Identity: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport.
- Proof of Address: Aadhaar Card, Water Bill, Electricity Bill.
- Passport size photograph.
Fees: None (Free of cost).
Steps to Apply:
1. Visit the Voter Service Portal (voters.eci.gov.in).
2. Click on 'New Registration for General Electors' (Form 6).
3. Fill in personal details, address, and upload documents.
4. Submit and note the Reference ID for tracking.
5. Booth Level Officer (BLO) will visit for field verification.
Official Portal: https://voters.eci.gov.in
Sources: https://eci.gov.in/voters/voter-electronic-guide-epic/
Authority: Election Commission of India.
