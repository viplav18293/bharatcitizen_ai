Title: Indian Passport Application
Overview: An Indian passport is issued by the Ministry of External Affairs to Indian citizens for the purpose of international travel.
Eligibility: Citizens of India can apply for a passport. Minors and adults have different requirements.
Required Documents:
- Proof of Address: Aadhaar Card, Water Bill, Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement.
- Proof of Date of Birth: Birth Certificate, PAN Card, Aadhaar Card.
- For Non-ECR: 10th standard passing certificate.
Fees:
- Fresh/Renewal (36 pages): ₹1500.
- Fresh/Renewal (60 pages): ₹2000.
- Tatkaal: Additional ₹2000.
Steps to Apply:
1. Register on the Passport Seva Portal.
2. Login and click 'Apply for Fresh Passport/Re-issue of Passport'.
3. Fill the application form and submit.
4. Click 'Pay and Schedule Appointment' to book a slot at a PSK (Passport Seva Kendra).
5. Visit the PSK with original documents for verification.
6. Police verification will follow.
Processing Time: 
- Normal: 30-45 days.
- Tatkaal: 1-3 days (after police verification).
Official Portal: https://www.passportindia.gov.in
Sources: https://www.passportindia.gov.in/AppOnlineProject/welcomeLink
Authority: Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India.
