Title: Driving License (DL) in India
Overview: A Driving License is an official document issued by the Government of India permitting individuals to operate various types of motor vehicles.
Eligibility: 
- 16 years (for 50cc engine capacity without gear).
- 18 years (for light motor vehicles with gear).
- 20 years (for transport vehicles).
Required Documents:
- Proof of Age: Birth Certificate, 10th Class Certificate, PAN Card.
- Proof of Address: Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Ration Card.
- Medical Certificate (Form 1A) for transport vehicles or applicants over 40.
Fees:
- Learner's License: ₹150 + ₹50 (test fee).
- Permanent License: ₹200 + ₹300 (test fee).
Steps to Apply:
1. Visit the Sarathi Parivahan portal.
2. Apply for 'Learner License' first.
3. Fill the form, upload documents, and pay fee.
4. Book slot for LL test. Pass the test to get LL.
5. After 30 days of LL, apply for 'Driving License'.
6. Book slot for driving test. Pass the test to get DL.
Official Portal: https://parivahan.gov.in
