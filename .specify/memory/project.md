# Project Name: BharatAI Citizen Assistant

## Purpose:
AI-powered Indian citizen copilot utilizing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide grounded, source-backed information on Indian government services, schemes, and rights.

## Main Features:
- Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License assistance.
- Government Scheme guidance (PM Kisan, Ayushman Bharat, PMAY).
- Legal and Rights assistance (RTI, Labour, Consumer Rights).
- Exam and Career Guidance (UPSC, JEE, NEET, SSC).

## Goals:
- Source-backed, verifiable answers from official domains.
- Elimination of hallucinations via RAG.
- Clear official link attribution.
- Professional, civic-tech grade reliability.
